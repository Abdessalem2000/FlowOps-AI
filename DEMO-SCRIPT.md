# AI Automation Agency - Demo Script

## Opening (1–2 minutes)

"Thanks for taking the time.
I'll quickly show you a simple lead dashboard I built for small agencies/ecommerce.
Goal is: centralize all leads from your forms, see quick stats, and manage statuses in one place."

## Walkthrough (5–7 minutes)

### 1. Show Landing Page
"This is where leads come from – your public forms."

### 2. Submit a Test Lead
"Let me submit a quick test lead so you can see it appear in real time."
*(Fill out the form with sample data)*

### 3. Go to Dashboard
"Here's your dashboard. You can see:"
- **Total leads** - All leads captured from your forms
- **Leads this week** - Recent activity
- **Pipeline summary** - Quick breakdown by status

### 4. Show New Lead
"See how the new lead appeared instantly in 'Latest leads in your pipeline'?"

### 5. Use Search & Filters
"If you have many leads, you can:"
- **Search** by name or email
- **Filter** by status (New, In progress, Closed)
- **Combine** both for precise results

### 6. Change Lead Status
"Click the status dropdown to update lead pipeline stage."
*(Change a lead from 'New' to 'In progress')*
"Notice how the pipeline summary updates automatically?"

### 7. Add Manual Lead
"You can also add leads manually:"
*(Click 'Add test lead' button)*
"Perfect for leads from phone calls, events, or manual entry."

### 8. Pipeline Summary
"Here's your quick pipeline summary: X new, Y in progress, Z closed."
- **Green (New)** - Fresh leads ready to contact
- **Blue (In progress)** - Active conversations
- **Gray (Closed)** - Completed or lost deals

## Close & Next Step (2–3 minutes)

"If this fits your workflow, I can:"
- **Customize forms** to match your brand
- **Adjust statuses** to match your sales process
- **Add fields** specific to your business
- **Connect to your tools** (email, CRM, calendar)
- **Set up automations** for follow-ups

"Would you like me to set up a version of this just for your agency and plug in your real forms?"

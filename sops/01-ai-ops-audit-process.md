# AI Ops Audit Process SOP

## Purpose
Standard process for conducting AI Ops Audit calls to identify automation opportunities and create implementation roadmaps.

## Pre-Audit Preparation

### 1. Research Phase (30 minutes before call)
- [ ] Review company website and LinkedIn profile
- [ ] Identify current tools and tech stack
- [ ] Research industry benchmarks
- [ ] Prepare personalized questions based on their business
- [ ] Set up screen sharing for demo

### 2. Tools Setup
- [ ] Open Notion template for audit notes
- [ ] Prepare hero workflow demo environment
- [ ] Test calendar booking link
- [ ] Have ROI calculator ready

## Audit Call Structure (60-90 minutes)

### Phase 1: Introduction (5 minutes)
**Objective:** Build rapport and set expectations

**Script:**
"Hi [Name], thanks for meeting today. I'm [Your Name] and I help B2B teams automate lead handling, onboarding, and support with AI agents.

In our 60 minutes together, we'll:
1. Understand your current processes
2. Identify 2-3 quick automation wins  
3. Show you a demo of what's possible
4. Leave you with a clear roadmap and ROI estimate

Sound good?"

### Phase 2: Discovery (20-25 minutes)
**Objective:** Map current processes and pain points

#### Current Process Questions
**Lead Handling:**
- "How do leads currently contact you?" (website, email, referrals)
- "Who handles lead qualification?" (person, process)
- "What's your average response time to new leads?"
- "How many leads do you get per week?"
- "What's your current lead-to-call booking rate?"

**Onboarding:**
- "Walk me through your client onboarding process"
- "How long does it typically take from signed contract to fully onboarded?"
- "What are the common bottlenecks or delays?"
- "Who's involved in the onboarding process?"

**Support:**
- "How do customers currently get support?" (email, phone, chat)
- "What are your most common support questions?"
- "What's your average response time for support inquiries?"
- "How much time does your team spend on support weekly?"

#### Pain Point Identification
- "What's the most frustrating manual task you do regularly?"
- "Where do you feel you're losing leads or customers?"
- "If you could automate one process tomorrow, what would it be?"
- "How many hours per week does your team spend on repetitive tasks?"

### Phase 3: Process Mapping (10-15 minutes)
**Objective:** Visualize current vs future state

**Current State Mapping:**
- Draw current workflow on screen
- Identify manual handoffs and bottlenecks
- Note time delays and pain points
- Calculate current time investment

**Future State Vision:**
- Show automated workflow alternatives
- Highlight time savings at each step
- Demonstrate improved customer experience
- Calculate potential ROI

### Phase 4: Demo (15-20 minutes)
**Objective:** Show concrete automation capabilities

**Hero Workflow Demo:**
"Let me show you exactly how this would work for your business."

1. **Lead Intake Demo**
   - Show AI chat widget collecting information
   - Demonstrate qualification logic
   - Show real-time CRM entry

2. **Calendar Booking**
   - Demonstrate automatic availability checking
   - Show calendar event creation
   - Display confirmation emails

3. **Analytics Dashboard**
   - Show response time metrics
   - Display qualification accuracy
   - Demonstrate ROI tracking

### Phase 5: ROI Analysis (10 minutes)
**Objective:** Quantify business impact

**Time Savings Calculation:**
```
Current Process:
- Lead qualification: X hours/week × $Y/hour = $Z/week
- Onboarding: A hours/week × $B/hour = $C/week  
- Support: D hours/week × $E/hour = $F/week

Total Current Cost: $Z + $C + $F = $Total/week
Annual Cost: $Total × 52 = $Annual

Automated Process:
- 80% time reduction = $Annual × 0.8 = $Savings
- Implementation cost: $X
- Payback period: $X ÷ $Savings = Y months
```

**Additional Benefits:**
- 24/7 availability
- Consistent customer experience
- Better data and insights
- Scalable growth

### Phase 6: Next Steps (5 minutes)
**Objective:** Define clear path forward

**Options:**
1. **Free POC** (for select prospects)
   - Implement one small workflow
   - 2-week timeline
   - Demonstrate concrete results

2. **Paid Audit** ($2K-$5K)
   - Detailed process documentation
   - Technical implementation plan
   - Vendor selection guidance

3. **Implementation Package** ($10K-$25K)
   - Full workflow build
   - Team training
   - 30-day optimization

**Closing Script:**
"Based on our conversation, I see [specific opportunity] as your biggest quick win. 

We could implement this in [timeline] and save you [X hours/month], which translates to $[Y] in monthly value.

Which option makes the most sense for you right now?"

## Post-Audit Follow-up

### Immediate Actions (within 2 hours)
- [ ] Send thank-you email with summary
- [ ] Share demo recording link
- [ ] Include ROI calculation summary
- [ ] Propose next step based on discussion

### 24-Hour Follow-up
- [ ] Send detailed audit document (Notion/PDF)
- [ ] Include implementation timeline
- [ ] Provide case studies relevant to their industry
- [ ] Schedule next call if interested

### Weekly Follow-up (if no response)
- Week 1: "Any questions about the audit?"
- Week 2: "Thought I'd share this relevant case study"
- Week 3: "Quick question about your timeline for automation"
